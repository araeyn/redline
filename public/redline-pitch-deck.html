The Problem

Artists do not just need more generation. They need better direction.

  • Most AI art tools focus on replacing the creative process.
  • The real pain point is the middle of a piece, when an artist is unsure what to fix.
  • Feedback is often slow, vague, or disconnected from the artwork itself.
  • Creators waste hours rendering before they know if the piece even reads.

Big takeaway

The hardest part of making art is not starting. It is deciding what to change while the piece is still unfinished.

The key contrast is process versus output. Redline helps during the decision-making stage, when the work is still editable and artistic judgment matters most.
Slide 2The messy middle is underserved
The Product

Redline turns a WIP into an actionable mentor pass.

Critique
Places visual critique directly on the artwork instead of giving generic text feedback.
Relight
Lets artists audition stronger lighting directions before repainting highlights and shadows by hand.
Readability
Checks whether the idea still reads at thumbnail size using silhouette and edge simplification.
Focus Region
Boxes one weak area and runs a localized mentor pass instead of reanalyzing the whole image.
Layers
Breaks the image into structural masses so artists can see design instead of noise.
Next Pass Plan
Converts diagnosis into concrete, prioritized revision steps the artist can actually follow.

One sentence

Redline behaves like a digital studio partner: diagnose, simulate, interpret, and guide.

This is where you establish breadth. It is not a one-feature demo. It is a workflow tool built around how artists actually iterate.

Technical Depth

More than a wrapper. Redline combines interactive analysis with private AI critique.

  • Client-side vision passes power relighting, structural layers, framing, and readability tests.
  • Focus Region enables local mentor passes on just one selected part of the artwork.
  • Critique runs through a server-side route so the Gemini API key stays private.
  • The workspace adapts to portrait and landscape art and supports real-time interactions.

Engineering story

This feels like a product because it combines multiple visual models, interaction design, caching, region selection, and server-side critique orchestration.

This is where developer judges should nod. It is not only a prompt. It is a full creative analysis system with UI and systems work behind it.
